Joti-JOTA 2021

JOTA-JOTI is the world’s largest digital Scout event taking place online and over the air. The educational event brings together more than 2 million Scouts every year in October for a weekend of Scouting and friendship. Young people can learn about communications technology and connect with fellow Scouts from over 171 countries.

International Jamboree danemark 2022

danemark, summer 2022

Jamboree Denmark 2022 0 SHARES 2 COMMENTS Translate to... Saturday, July 23, 2022 - 12:00 to Sunday, July 31, 2022 - 12:00 Point with a center of latitude 55.622569 and longitude 12.182096. In 2022, 40.000 Danish and international Scouts and Guides from all over the world will be gathering in Hedeland at Europe’s biggest Jamboree of the year. The Jamboree site is located close to Copenhagen, the capital of Denmark. The Jamboree will offer fellowship, outdoor activities, and challenges to children, youth and adults. Hedeland is where water, nature and outdoor adventure meet and will provide unique opportunities for some cool and unexpected activities for Scouts and Guides. Apart from unique activities, the Scouts and Guides will sleep in tents, cook their own meals over open fire and will need to pioneer dinner tables etc. using only wooden pools and ropes. We are looking forward to seeing you.
